About A CyberLife

is a physics based 2d platformer with cloth simulations, set in a cyberlife city called Alpha City One.

Features

Discover the A CyberLife: is an action-platformer that combines fast-paced, skilled movement and combat with a dark and beautiful world. Indulge yourself in decadent levels, complete with their own weather systems and architectures. Journey through a cyberpunk dystopia, filled with corrupt mega-corporations and rogue AI. Difficult, acrobatic platforming awaits the brave souls who dare to take control of - a fusion powered killer android capable of firing high intensity laser beams from her eyes. You'll have to carefully choose which body mods to use, in order to survive and complete challenging levels. Have fun! More information
